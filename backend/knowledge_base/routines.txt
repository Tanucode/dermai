SKINCARE ROUTINES KNOWLEDGE BASE
==================================

ROUTINE ORDER (Golden Rule — Always Thinnest to Thickest):
Cleanser → Toner → Essence → Serum → Eye Cream → Moisturizer → Face Oil → SPF (morning only)

=============================
OILY SKIN MORNING ROUTINE
=============================
Step 1 - Gel Cleanser: Use a foaming or gel cleanser to remove excess overnight oil. Look for salicylic acid or tea tree in ingredients.
Step 2 - Toner (optional): Use an alcohol-free toner to balance skin pH. BHA toner works great for oily skin.
Step 3 - Niacinamide Serum: Apply 2-3 drops to control oil and reduce pores throughout the day.
Step 4 - Lightweight Gel Moisturizer: Even oily skin needs moisture. Skipping moisturizer makes skin produce MORE oil.
Step 5 - SPF 50+ Sunscreen: Use a matte or gel SPF to avoid looking greasy. This is NON-NEGOTIABLE.

=============================
OILY SKIN NIGHT ROUTINE
=============================
Step 1 - Oil Cleanser or Micellar Water: Remove sunscreen and pollution. Oil cleansers don't make skin oilier.
Step 2 - Gel Cleanser: Double cleanse to ensure clean skin for actives.
Step 3 - Exfoliant (2-3x per week only): Use salicylic acid (BHA) toner to unclog pores.
Step 4 - Niacinamide or Retinol Serum: Alternate nights — niacinamide 4 nights, retinol 3 nights.
Step 5 - Lightweight Moisturizer: Gel-cream texture works best.

=============================
DRY SKIN MORNING ROUTINE
=============================
Step 1 - Cream Cleanser: Use a gentle, non-stripping cream cleanser. Avoid foaming cleansers.
Step 2 - Hydrating Toner: Layer hyaluronic acid toner to prep skin for moisture.
Step 3 - Hyaluronic Acid Serum: Apply to damp skin immediately after toner for maximum absorption.
Step 4 - Vitamin C Serum: Brightens and protects. Apply after HA serum.
Step 5 - Rich Moisturizer with Ceramides: CeraVe Moisturizing Cream or similar.
Step 6 - Facial Oil (optional): Rosehip oil or squalane for extra nourishment.
Step 7 - SPF 50+: Use a hydrating SPF formula, not matte.

=============================
DRY SKIN NIGHT ROUTINE
=============================
Step 1 - Gentle Cream Cleanser: Do NOT double cleanse — it strips dry skin.
Step 2 - Essence or Hyaluronic Acid Toner: Layer moisture.
Step 3 - Retinol (2x per week only, start slow): Use lowest concentration and buffer with moisturizer.
Step 4 - Rich Moisturizer: Apply generously. Look for ceramides, shea butter, glycerin.
Step 5 - Sleeping Mask (2-3x per week): Vaseline or Laneige Water Sleeping Mask as final step.

=============================
COMBINATION SKIN ROUTINE
=============================
Morning:
Step 1 - Gel Cleanser (gentle)
Step 2 - Balancing Toner
Step 3 - Niacinamide Serum (balances both dry and oily areas)
Step 4 - Light Moisturizer
Step 5 - SPF

Night:
Step 1 - Micellar Water then Gel Cleanser
Step 2 - AHA Toner (glycolic acid) 2x per week
Step 3 - Hyaluronic Acid Serum
Step 4 - Lightweight Moisturizer

=============================
SENSITIVE SKIN ROUTINE
=============================
RULE: Patch test everything. Introduce ONE new product at a time. Wait 2 weeks before adding another.
Morning:
Step 1 - Very gentle, fragrance-free cleanser (Cetaphil, CeraVe Hydrating)
Step 2 - Calm Toner (skip if reactive)
Step 3 - Centella Asiatica or Ceramide Serum (calming)
Step 4 - Rich Ceramide Moisturizer
Step 5 - Mineral SPF (zinc oxide — less irritating than chemical SPF)

Night:
Step 1 - Gentle Cleanser
Step 2 - Azelaic Acid (gentler than retinol, treats acne + redness)
Step 3 - Ceramide Moisturizer

=============================
DARK CIRCLES SPECIFIC ROUTINE
=============================
Morning:
- Apply caffeine eye cream by gently patting (never rubbing) around orbital bone
- Use cold spoon or eye patches to depuff before application
- Apply Vitamin C serum on the area (brightens pigmentation)
- Always use SPF around eyes

Night:
- Apply retinol eye cream (very low concentration, 0.025-0.05%)
- Or use bakuchiol (natural retinol alternative, gentler for eye area)

Additional tips for dark circles:
- Sleep on your back with head elevated (reduces fluid pooling)
- 7-9 hours of quality sleep
- Reduce salt intake (causes puffiness)
- Stay hydrated
- Treat allergies (common cause of dark circles)

=============================
ACNE-PRONE SKIN SPECIFIC ROUTINE
=============================
Morning:
Step 1 - Salicylic acid or benzoyl peroxide cleanser
Step 2 - BHA toner (2-3x per week)
Step 3 - Niacinamide serum
Step 4 - Oil-free, non-comedogenic moisturizer
Step 5 - Non-comedogenic SPF

Night:
Step 1 - Double cleanse
Step 2 - BHA exfoliant (alternate nights)
Step 3 - Retinol (builds to this over weeks)
Step 4 - Lightweight gel moisturizer

Spot treatment for active pimples:
- Apply benzoyl peroxide 2.5% directly on pimple only
- Or use salicylic acid spot treatment
- Or use hydrocolloid pimple patches overnight (absorbs pus, reduces inflammation)
